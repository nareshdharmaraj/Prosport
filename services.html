Service Center FAQ

How long is the warranty?

All ProSport equipment comes with a standard 2-year warranty. Frame and structural components have a lifetime warranty.

Do you service other brands?

Yes, our certified technicians are trained to repair all major commercial gym equipment brands, though pricing may vary.

What is included in the annual inspection?

A 50-point check including cable tension, lubrication, electronic diagnostics, and structural bolt tightening.
